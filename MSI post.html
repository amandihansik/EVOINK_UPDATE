Project Overview

This marketing collateral piece was created for Abans IT to promote a specific MSI gaming laptop model. The design functions as a high-impact sales tool for social media, in-store displays, and promotional print materials. It effectively combines the product's technical specifications with a time-sensitive financial incentive (the HNB Offer) to appeal to gamers and power users looking for performance and affordability.

The design follows a Z-pattern layout. The eye is first drawn to the top-left MSI logo and the powerful red "HNB OFFER." It then moves diagonally across the product image and key specs in the center, before landing on the call-to-action and Abans IT information at the bottom right.

Design Rationale

The design prioritizes clarity and information accessibility. In the paint industry, consumers need quick access to technical specifications to make informed purchasing decisions. By organizing this information in a clean, visually appealing layout, the design serves both marketing and educational purposes—attracting attention while providing value.

The blue color palette was chosen strategically:

  • Incentive-First: The massive, red "HNB OFFER" and "0%" terms are the first thing a viewer sees. This immediately addresses a potential barrier to purchase (cost) and presents a solution.
  • Credibility Through Logos: By prominently featuring the official Intel and NVIDIA logos and detailed model numbers (i7, RTX 3050 Ti), the design builds trust with an audience that knows exactly what these specifications mean.
  • Energy and Vibe: The term "SUCHWAVE" (a likely stylized take on "tech wave" or "shockwave"), combined with the glitch text effect, creates a sense of energy, speed, and cutting-edge technology that resonates with the gaming community.
