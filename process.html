Process FAQs

Common questions about how we work

Timelines vary by project scope. A logo design typically takes 3-4 weeks, while a full brand identity takes 8-10 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific needs.

Your involvement is crucial at key milestones: initial briefing, concept review, and final approval. We'll guide you through exactly what input we need at each stage. Most clients spend 2-3 hours total across the project providing feedback.

We offer unlimited revisions until you're completely satisfied. Our process includes structured feedback rounds to ensure we're always moving in the right direction without endless back-and-forth.

That's why we start with multiple concepts! If none of the initial directions resonate, we'll go back to the strategy phase and explore new directions until we find the perfect fit. Your satisfaction is guaranteed.

We use a combination of tools based on your preference: email for updates, video calls for presentations, and project management tools (like Trello or Asana) for tracking progress and feedback.

You'll receive all source files (AI, PSD, Figma) and final exports in multiple formats (JPG, PNG, PDF, SVG) optimized for print, web, and social media. Plus comprehensive brand guidelines explaining how to use everything.
