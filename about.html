Our Story

How EvoInk came to be and what drives us forward

The Beginning

EvoInk was born from a simple observation: great brands don't stay the same—they evolve. Founded in 2020, we started as a one-person design studio with a passion for creating meaningful visual identities that could grow and adapt with businesses.

The Philosophy

We believe that design is a strategic tool for business growth. Every color choice, every typography decision, every pixel placement serves a purpose. Our name reflects this philosophy—"Evo" for evolution, "Ink" for the mark we leave on the world.

Today

What started as a solo venture has grown into a collaborative studio working with clients across industries and continents. We've helped over 50 brands evolve their identity, from local startups to established companies seeking renewal.
