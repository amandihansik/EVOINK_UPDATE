Project Overview

This marketing collateral piece was created for Dulux Weathershield Powerflexx, a premium exterior paint product. The design serves as both a social media graphic and a point-of-sale informational post, communicating the product's unique selling proposition—"Unique Multi Protection"—while presenting technical specifications in an accessible, visually appealing format.

Design Rationale

The design prioritizes clarity and information accessibility. In the paint industry, consumers need quick access to technical specifications to make informed purchasing decisions. By organizing this information in a clean, visually appealing layout, the design serves both marketing and educational purposes—attracting attention while providing value.

The blue color palette was chosen strategically:

  • Deep blues communicate trust, reliability, and durability—key attributes for exterior paint
  • Gradient effects add visual interest and modern appeal
  • White text ensures maximum readability against the dark background
